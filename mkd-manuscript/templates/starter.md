## Chapter One

The train came in under rain and smoke.

Mara stood on the platform with one suitcase and a letter she had not yet opened.

She had come north for work, or that was what she had told everyone.
The truth was smaller and stranger.
She had come because a voice on the telephone had said, "Your father left something in the orchard, and it is waking up."

### Three Days Later

By morning, the station had become a town of rumors.

---

When she finally reached the orchard gate, the branches were moving though there was no wind.

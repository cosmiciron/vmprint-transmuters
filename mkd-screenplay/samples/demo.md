## INT. PLATFORM 9 - NIGHT

Wind tears through the station.
An arrival board flickers between DELAYED and a warning no one can read.

> @ELIAS
> If we miss this one, we wait six months.

> @RIN
> Then we do not miss it.

> @ELIAS (whispering)
> The rail is humming again.

> @RIN
> (under her breath)
> That means it is already here.

INT. SERVICE TUNNEL - LATER

> @ELIAS^
> We still have time.

> @RIN^
> Then stop talking and move.

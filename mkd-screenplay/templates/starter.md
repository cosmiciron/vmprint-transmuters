# LAST TRAIN TO ORBIT
- written by: J. Mercer
- draft date: 2026-03-09
- email: jmercer@example.com

## INT. PLATFORM 9 - NIGHT

Wind tears through the station.
An arrival board flickers between DELAYED and CANCELLED and something that looks like a warning.

> @ELIAS
> If we miss this one, we wait six months.

> @RIN
> Then we do not miss it.

The train arrives without a sound.

### CUT TO:

## EXT. SERVICE YARD - LATER

Blue sparks crawl along the rails.

> @ELIAS^
> We still have time.

> @RIN^
> Then stop talking.
